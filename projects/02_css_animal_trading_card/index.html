Tyrannosaurus Rex (T. rex)

Since it was first described in 1905, T. rex has become the most widely recognized dinosaur species in popular culture.

  • Name: T. rex
  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Aongest length: 12.3 m
  • Average height: 3.6 m
  • Average weight: 14 tons

Tyrannosaurus had their upper teeth covered in lips, instead of bare teeth as seen in crocodilians.
